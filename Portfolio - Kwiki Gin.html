KWIKI GIN - KIWI INFUSED GIN

A collection of product renders/promos for social media content and online ads

PROJECT DETAILS

CLIENT: KWIKI GIN

DATE: ONGOING

CATEGORY: VIDEOS / SOCIAL MEDIA / 3D MODELLING - ANIMATION / LIQUID, FIRE, SMOKE SIMULATIONS / PARTICLE SIMULATIONS

KWIKI GIN

I have had the privilege of creating captivating visuals for a thrilling rave experience, specifically designed for projection on a screen behind the DJ booth. These visuals were meticulously crafted to immerse the audience in a kaleidoscope of vibrant colors and mesmerizing patterns. I aimed to enhance the overall atmosphere of the event, creating a visually stimulating journey that perfectly complemented the sonic experience. Through the seamless integration of captivating visuals, I aimed to transport the audience into a world of electrifying audiovisual synergy, taking their rave experience to a whole new level.

Social Media Posts

In addition to creating stunning visuals for raves, I also had the opportunity to control the creation of engaging event posters for these events. With my understanding of the rave culture, I strategically crafted enticing content that resonated with the target audience. Through compelling graphics and strategically timed posts, I effectively generated buzz and anticipation around the events. With my social media marketing designs, I aimed to connect and engage with the vibrant rave community, fostering a sense of excitement and unity surrounding these unforgettable events.
