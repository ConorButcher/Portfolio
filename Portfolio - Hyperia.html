HYPERIA - THORPE PARK - A VISION BROUGHT TO LIFE

MARKETING 3D VISUALS FOR THE UK'S TALLEST AND FASTEST ROLLERCOASTER

PROJECT DETAILS

CLIENT: THORPE PARK

DATE: 2024

CATEGORY: EXTERIOR

Hyperia - A Vision Brought To Life

In my free time, I embarked on an exciting project to create photo-realistic images of the UK's new tallest and fastest roller coaster, Hyperia, located at Thorpe Park. This endeavor allowed me to explore various creative angles, producing three distinct versions of each angle captured during Daytime, Sunrise and Nighttime. These images aimed to encapsulate the thrilling essence of Hyperia, showcasing the ride in its full glory.


Collaboration and Recognition

After completing my initial renderings, I shared them with renowned theme park enthusiast Jack Silkstone. Impressed by the quality and attention to detail, Jack connected me with James, Thorpe Park's Brand Manager. This collaboration opened the door for my work to be featured in their marketing campaigns. My images were utilized across various platforms including social media, the Thorpe Park website, email newsletters, and advertisements, significantly contributing to the visual identity of Hyperia.



A Showcase of Skill and Passion

Creating these images was not just a technical exercise, but a passion project that demonstrated my commitment to high-quality visual art. The realistic depictions of Hyperia at different times of the day highlighted my ability to capture and enhance the dynamic atmosphere of this thrilling attraction. This project stands as a testament to my skills in digital rendering and my ability to contribute meaningfully to major marketing initiatives.



Bringing Hyperia to the World

Through this project, I was able to bring the excitement of Hyperia to life, allowing potential visitors to experience the ride's grandeur and thrill visually before even stepping foot in the park. The collaboration with Thorpe Park's marketing team ensured that my work reached a wide audience, playing a key role in the promotional efforts for the newest addition to the park's lineup. This experience has been immensely rewarding, solidifying my passion for creating immersive and realistic digital art.
